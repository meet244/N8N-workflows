# ü§ñ Automated Lead Generation & AI-Personalized Outreach (n8n Flow)

<img width="100%" alt="flow-graph" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a0a2e5b6-38d8-4e8f-81ff-1a09c2fbc600" />

This project is a comprehensive, two-part automation workflow built in **n8n** to streamline B2B lead generation and cold email outreach. It automatically scrapes targeted lead information from **Apollo.io**, populates a **Google Sheet**, and then uses **Google Gemini** to draft highly personalized outreach emails for each lead.

The entire system is designed for sales and marketing teams to build a scalable outreach engine that runs on autopilot, turning targeted searches into engagement-ready leads.

## üìå Features

  * üîé **Targeted Lead Sourcing**: Scrapes leads from **Apollo.io** based on a specific, detailed search URL.
  * ü§ñ **AI-Powered Personalization**: Uses **Google Gemini** to analyze lead data (title, industry, company keywords) and write a unique, friendly ice-breaker for each email.
  * üìä **Automated Data Management**: Cleans, maps, and stores lead data directly into a **Google Sheet**.
  * ‚è∞ **Scheduled Outreach Prep**: Automatically processes new leads and prepares personalized emails on a schedule.
  * üßπ **Data Cleansing**: Filters out leads without verified email addresses and removes duplicates to ensure a high-quality list.
  * ‚öôÔ∏è **Customizable & Scalable**: Easily change the target audience by modifying the Apollo search URL in the workflow.

-----

## üß© Flow Breakdown

The system is split into two distinct workflows: **Lead Sourcing** and **Personalized Email Outreach**.

### 1\. **Lead Sourcing (Manual Trigger)**

  * **Trigger**: Starts when a user clicks 'Test workflow'.
  * **Lead Configuration**: A **Set** node defines the target audience using a specific **Apollo.io search URL**. This URL contains filters for job titles, company size, industry keywords, and location.
  * **Scrape Apollo**: An **HTTP Request** node calls the **Apify API** to run an Apollo scraper, extracting a list of leads that match the search criteria.
  * **Data Processing**:
      * The raw response is mapped to a clean, structured format.
      * A **Filter** node removes any leads that do not have a verified email address.
      * A **Remove Duplicates** node ensures each lead is unique.
  * **Add to Sheet**: The cleaned, structured lead data is appended as new rows to a **Google Sheet**, ready for the next stage.

-----

### 2\. **Personalized Email Outreach (Scheduled Trigger)**

  * **Schedule Trigger**: This workflow runs automatically at a set interval (e.g., daily).
  * **Get Pending Leads**: It reads the **Google Sheet** and fetches all leads that have not yet had an email prepared.
  * **AI Email Generation (Gemini)**: For each lead, it sends their details (name, title, company info, etc.) to **Google Gemini** with a specific prompt to write a short, warm, and personalized "ice-breaker" sentence.
  * **Update Sheets**: The AI-generated personalized text is combined with a standard email template and saved back into the "Email Prepared" column in the Google Sheet for the corresponding lead.
